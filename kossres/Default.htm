Ground-Breaking Ceremony
 October 22, 1999

We officially broke ground on our new house construction project.  The architects hosted a ground breaking ceremony at the property that (foggy) Friday.  We hope to move in around May June 2001.

We now have a firm move-in scheduled for 5 pm on June 15, 2001.
