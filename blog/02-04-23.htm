Tuesday, April 23, 2002

Had lunch with Tony today.  The sun came back out (finally!) so decided to get some bike miles in (20 miles total).  This ride was my first to use the new clipless pedals I had installed on my Ryan Vanguard recumbent bike.  I'm glad to say I never dumped the bike (though I did run off the trail after hitting a bunch of "root-bumps" on the trail between Yarrow and Hunts points).

This was also my first ride using the Polar S-710 heart rate monitor.  This incredible device, will record your heart rate, bike speed, cadence, and altitude.  It can record a measurement every 5 seconds, and then upload your exercise profile to your PC via an infrared interface.

I haven't yet hooked up the speed and cadence - so the graph below just displays my heart rate and altitude for my trip from home to Microsoft.  Note you can see the elevation gain of about 500 ft between our house and the trip mid-point (at Bridle Trails).
